# Ollie's Kimchi - Product Requirements Document

## Overview

**Product:** E-commerce website for Ollie's Kimchi
**URL:** https://ollieskimchi.co.uk
**Owner:** Ollie (London-based artisan kimchi producer)

## Business Context

Ollie's Kimchi is a premium, handcrafted kimchi brand made in London using traditional Korean fermentation methods. The brand was featured in The Guardian's kimchi taste test as **"Best All-Rounder"** with **5 stars**.

### Value Proposition
- Naturally fermented (no shortcuts, no pasteurisation)
- Live cultures / probiotics
- Made fresh in London
- Vegan-friendly
- Award-winning taste (Guardian 5-star review)

## Target Audience

- Health-conscious consumers interested in fermented foods
- Foodies seeking authentic, high-quality kimchi
- UK-based customers (free delivery over £35)
- People looking to support small/artisan food producers
